Day 5

Ngong Ping 360 + Big Buddha + Tai O Village

🚠 Ngong Ping 360 Cable Car
📍 Ngong Ping 360, Lantau Island
Breathtaking cable car ride over the mountains and coastline to Lantau's plateau. Spectacular views!
🎟️ Book tickets in advance online!
🏯 Explore Ngong Ping Village
Take your time exploring these iconic landmarks:
  • ⛩️ Po Lin Monastery — tranquil Buddhist monastery
  • 🗿 Tian Tan Buddha (Big Buddha) — climb 268 steps to the base!
🌊 Tai O Fishing Village
📍 Tai O, Lantau Island
Step back in time at this charming stilt-house fishing village. Wander the lanes, try local snacks, and watch the stilted homes over the waterway. Very photogenic!
🍽️ Dinner on the way back
📍 Citygate Outlets, Tung Chung
Stop for dinner at Citygate Outlets near the MTR before heading back to the city.
👟
Wear comfy shoes! There's a lot of walking today — the Big Buddha has 268 steps and Tai O involves cobblestones. Start early to beat the crowds and midday heat.

Quick Reference

Travel Essentials 📌

Getting Around
🚇
MTR (Subway)
Fastest & cheapest way around Hong Kong. Get an Octopus Card for easy tapping.
🚖
Taxis
Red taxis cover urban areas. Metered and generally affordable. Have hotel address in Chinese.
⛴️
Star Ferry
Scenic & cheap crossing between Kowloon and HK Island. One of the world's best ferry rides!
🚠
Peak Tram
Historic tram to Victoria Peak. Book tickets online to skip long queues.
Hotels
🏰 Disney Explorer's Lodge (Days 1–3)
📍 Lantau Island, near Disneyland
Great themed hotel with gardens, playground, pool. ~15–20 min taxi from airport.
🏙️ BP International (Days 3–7)
📍 Jordan, Kowloon
Central location for city exploration. Near Temple Street Night Market. ~25–30 min from Disneyland.
Must-Eat List 🍽️
🦆 Yat Lok Roast Goose 🍜 Mak Man Kee Noodle Shop 🥟 DimDimSum Specialty ⭐ Tim Ho Wan (Michelin) 🥟 One Dim Sum 🍲 Hing Kee Claypot Rice 🍳 Australian Dairy Company
Money Tips 💰
💳
Currency: HKD (Hong Kong Dollar)
Get an Octopus Card for MTR, buses, ferries — and even 7-Eleven! Exchange cash at the airport or licensed money changers in the city (avoid hotel rates). Most malls accept cards.
Weather & Packing ☀️
👜
Bring comfortable walking shoes (lots of walking!), light layers for air-conditioned malls, sunscreen & hats for outdoor days, and a compact umbrella. Stay hydrated — especially on the Ngong Ping / Tai O day!
