Step-by-Step Directions

Transport Guide 🚌

Every journey in your trip, from start to finish. Tap any route to expand the directions. Get an Octopus Card on Day 1 at the airport — it works on all MTR, buses, and the Star Ferry throughout the trip.
Day 1 · Arrival
Day 1 ✈️ Airport → Disney Explorer's Lodge ▼
  • 1Exit Arrivals Hall at Hong Kong International Airport (HKIA). Head to the Taxi Stand outside the terminal.
  • 2Join the taxi queue. Look for a red urban taxi or a green Lantau taxi — both can take you to Disneyland.
  • 3Tell the driver: "Disney Explorer's Lodge" or show: 迪士尼探索家度假酒店
  • 4The taxi will take the North Lantau Highway directly to the hotel. ~15–20 min ~HKD 120–150
  • 5Pay by cash (HKD) or Octopus Card. ⚠️ Buy Octopus Card at the airport before leaving!
Day 2 & 3 · Disneyland
Day 2 & 3 🏰 Disney Explorer's Lodge → Disneyland Park ▼
  • 1Walk from Disney Explorer's Lodge to the Disney Resort Shuttle Bus stop outside the hotel lobby.
  • 2Board the free Disney Resort shuttle to Hong Kong Disneyland main entrance. ~5–8 min Free
  • 3Alternatively, walk the scenic path from the resort to the park entrance. ~15 min walk
  • 4Show your tickets at the main gate. Download the HK Disneyland app for ride wait times and show schedules.
Day 3 🧳 Disney Explorer's Lodge → BP International (Jordan) ▼
  • 1Collect luggage from the hotel front desk (stored since morning). Go to the taxi stand outside the hotel.
  • 2Take a red urban taxi. Show the driver: "BP International Hotel, Jordan" or 佐敦柏麗酒店
  • 3Route goes via Lantau Link / Tsing Ma Bridge through the tunnel into Kowloon. ~25–30 min ~HKD 250–300
  • 4⚠️ Note: Tunnel toll is extra (~HKD 45) — driver will add it to the fare.
  • 5Drop off at BP International, 8 Austin Road, Jordan. Check in at the front desk.
Day 4 · The Peak, Ferry & Harbour
Day 4 🏨 BP International → Peak Tram Station ▼
  • 1Option A — Taxi (Easiest): Hail a red taxi outside the hotel. Tell driver: "Peak Tram Lower Terminus, Garden Road" or 山頂纜車總站，花園道. ~20–25 min ~HKD 80–100
  • 2Option B — MTR + Walk: Walk to Jordan MTR Station (3 min). Take Tsuen Wan Line (red line) to Central Station. ~15 min ~HKD 10
  • 3At Central, exit via Exit J2. Walk up Garden Road toward the Peak Tram terminal (~10 min uphill walk), or take Bus 15C from Edinburgh Place. ~HKD 5
  • 4At the Peak Tram Lower Terminus: queue up, buy tickets (or use pre-booked tickets). ⚠️ Book online in advance to avoid long queues!
Day 4 🚋 Peak Tram Up & Down Victoria Peak ▼
  • 1Going UP: Board the Peak Tram at the Lower Terminus (Garden Road). The tram runs every 10–15 min. ~8 min ride ~HKD 52–68 return
  • 2Alight at Peak Tower (Upper Terminus). Head to the Sky Terrace 428 or walk Lugard Road for free views.
  • 3Going DOWN: Best to take a taxi from the Peak down to Central/Sheung Wan. Faster and easier with kids. ~15 min ~HKD 60–80
  • 4Alternative down: Bus 15 from the Peak to Central (Exchange Square Bus Terminus). ~30 min ~HKD 11
Day 4 ⛴️ Central → Tsim Sha Tsui via Star Ferry ▼
  • 1After lunch at Yat Lok, walk west along Des Voeux Road toward Central Ferry Piers. Follow signs for Pier 7 (Star Ferry). ~10–15 min walk
  • 2At the ferry pier, tap your Octopus Card at the turnstile to board. Ferries depart every 6–12 min. ~HKD 3.4 (lower deck)
  • 3Enjoy the 10-min harbour crossing! Stay on the upper deck if possible — best views of both skylines. ~10 min
  • 4Alight at Tsim Sha Tsui Star Ferry Pier. The Clock Tower is right there at the exit — great photo spot!
Day 4 🛍️ Clock Tower → K11 Musea ▼
  • 1From the Clock Tower, walk east along Salisbury Road toward K11 Musea. ~8–10 min walk
  • 2K11 Musea is at 18 Salisbury Road, Tsim Sha Tsui — a distinctive waterfront building. Look for the large "K11 MUSEA" signage.
  • 3Alternative if tired: grab a taxi right outside the ferry pier. ~3 min ~HKD 25
Day 4 🚖 Avenue of Stars → BP International ▼
  • 1After Symphony of Lights (~8:10 PM), walk back toward the main road on Salisbury Road.
  • 2Option A — Taxi: Hail a red taxi on Salisbury Road. Tell driver: "BP International Hotel, Austin Road, Jordan". ~10 min ~HKD 30–40
  • 3Option B — MTR: Walk to Tsim Sha Tsui MTR Station (~5 min). Take Tsuen Wan Line (red) one stop to Jordan Station. Exit Exit A, walk 3 min to hotel. ~HKD 4
Day 5 · Ngong Ping & Tai O
Day 5 🚇 BP International → Tung Chung (for Cable Car) ▼
  • 1Walk 3 min from hotel to Jordan MTR Station. Enter using Octopus Card.
  • 2Take the Tsuen Wan Line (Red) toward Tsuen Wan. Ride 2 stops to Lai King Station.
  • 3At Lai King, transfer to the Tung Chung Line (Orange). Take it to the terminus: Tung Chung Station. ~50 min total ~HKD 20
  • 4At Tung Chung, exit the station and follow signs to Ngong Ping 360 Cable Car terminal (~5 min walk).
  • 5Show pre-booked tickets or buy at the counter. ⚠️ Book cable car tickets online in advance — queues can be very long!
Day 5 🚠 Ngong Ping Cable Car Ride ▼
  • 1Board the cable car at Tung Chung Terminal. Standard gondolas fit 8 people; Crystal Cabins have a glass floor (great for kids!). ~25 min ride
  • 2Arrive at Ngong Ping Village Terminal. Walk 5 min to Po Lin Monastery and the Big Buddha.
  • 3Return ride at end of day — keep your return ticket. Last cable car is around 6:00 PM.
Day 5 🚌 Ngong Ping → Tai O Fishing Village ▼
  • 1From Ngong Ping Village, walk to the Ngong Ping Bus Terminus (2 min from the cable car terminal).
  • 2Board Bus 21 (Ngong Ping → Tai O). Runs every 20–30 min. Pay with Octopus Card. ~20–25 min ~HKD 5
  • 3Alight at Tai O Bus Terminus — the village is right there. Explore the stilt houses and waterway lanes.
Day 5 🚌 Tai O → Tung Chung (for Dinner + MTR) ▼
  • 1From Tai O Bus Terminus, board Bus 11 (Tai O → Tung Chung). Runs every 20–30 min. ~35–40 min ~HKD 7
  • 2Alight at Tung Chung Bus Terminus. Walk 5 min to Citygate Outlets for dinner.
  • 3After dinner, walk to Tung Chung MTR Station. Take the Tung Chung Line (Orange) all the way back, transfer at Lai King to Tsuen Wan Line (Red), ride to Jordan Station. ~50 min ~HKD 20
  • 4At Jordan Station, take Exit A and walk 3 min to BP International.
Day 6 · Museum, Shopping & Night Market
Day 6 🔬 BP International → Hong Kong Science Museum ▼
  • 1Walk from hotel to Jordan MTR Station (~3 min). Take Tsuen Wan Line (Red) to Tsim Sha Tsui Station (1 stop). ~HKD 4
  • 2Exit via Exit P1 or P2. Walk along Chatham Road South toward Science Museum Road. ~10 min walk
  • 3HK Science Museum is at 2 Science Museum Road, Tsim Sha Tsui East. Open 10 AM–7 PM (closed Thu).
Day 6 🛍️ Science Museum → Mira Place (shopping) ▼
  • 1Walk north from the museum toward Granville Road. Mira Place is at 132 Nathan Road, Tsim Sha Tsui. ~12 min walk
  • 2Alternatively, take a short taxi from the museum. ~5 min ~HKD 25
Day 6 👟 Mira Place → Fa Yuen St (Sneaker Street) — Optional ▼
  • 1Walk north on Nathan Road from Mira Place. Turn right into Fa Yuen Street (near Mong Kok area). ~15–20 min walk
  • 2Or take the MTR from Tsim Sha Tsui to Mong Kok Station (Tsuen Wan Line, 2 stops). Exit Exit D3. ~HKD 5 ~5 min
Day 6 🌙 Hotel → Temple Street Night Market ▼
  • 1Temple Street Night Market is very close to BP International — it's in the same Jordan neighbourhood!
  • 2Walk north from the hotel on Temple Street (~5 min walk). The market starts from Tin Hau Temple and stretches north toward Jordan Road.
  • 3Best time to visit: after 7:00 PM when all stalls are fully set up and buzzing!
  • 4To return: simply walk back south on Temple Street to the hotel. ~5 min walk Free
Day 7 · Departure
Day 7 🍳 Hotel → Australian Dairy Company (Breakfast) ▼
  • 1Australian Dairy Company is at 47–49 Parkes Street, Jordan — a 5-min walk from BP International.
  • 2Walk north on Austin Road, turn left on Parkes Street. Look for the queue outside — it's always busy! ⚠️ Arrive early, queues form fast
Day 7 ✈️ BP International → Hong Kong Airport (Departure) ▼
  • 1Option A — Taxi (Easiest with luggage): Hail a red taxi outside the hotel. Tell driver: "Hong Kong International Airport" or 香港國際機場. ~35–45 min ~HKD 280–330 +tunnel toll ~HKD 45
  • 2Option B — Airport Express (Recommended): Walk or taxi (~10 min) to Kowloon Station on Austin Road. Board the Airport Express (AEL) direct to HKIA. ~24 min ~HKD 105/adult
  • 3💡 Pro tip: Use the In-Town Check-in service at Kowloon Station — check your bags in and get your boarding pass before boarding the train!
  • 4For a 1:40 PM flight, leave the hotel no later than 9:45–10:00 AM to allow time for check-in, immigration, and security.
🪙
Octopus Card reminder: Works on all MTR trains, buses, Star Ferry, minibuses, trams, and even convenience stores. Top it up at any MTR station Customer Service Centre or Add Value Machine.

👉 Closest to your hotel + best food cluster early. Just walk straight — no zigzag, one straight line south through the market.
Stop 1 — Main Meal First 🍲
🍲
Hing Kee Claypot Rice ⭐ Order: Sausage + chicken claypot rice · Add egg · 1 pot good for sharing
⏱️ Stay 30–40 mins · 💡 Go early — line builds fast!
Stop 2 — Seafood Sharing 🦪
🦪
Garlic Scallops + Clams or Oysters Walk 2–3 min deeper into the street. Order 1–2 dishes only — snacks still ahead!
Stop 3 — Quick Noodles 🍜 (Optional)
🍜
Beef Chow Fun OR Soy Sauce Noodles Get 1 plate to share · Choose stall with flames + long line = better taste 🔥
Stop 4 — Walk & Snack Zone 🦑
🍢
Curry Fish Balls (must for kids 👍) Interactive + casual — the fun part for your kid!
🦑
Grilled Squid or Skewers Buy small bites as you walk — slow down and just nibble
Stop 5 — Dessert Finish 🍮
🥭
Mango Sago Refreshing after all the salty food — near middle/south end
🍮
Tofu Pudding Light option — also great for kids
💡 Backup dessert: 佳佳甜品 (Kai Kai Dessert) — very famous, short walk off Temple Street. Great if street stalls are too crowded.
⏱️ Time Plan
6:30 PM → Start at Jordan Road entrance
7:15 PM → Finish main meal
8:00 PM → Snacks + dessert
8:30 PM → Done — perfect before it gets too crowded 👍
🚶 Exit + Walk Back to Hotel
Exit near Jordan MTR Exit A → walk along Austin Road straight to hotel. ⏱️ ~8–12 min, flat, well-lit, safe.

🚕 Backup (if tired): step out to Jordan Road, grab a taxi. ~3–5 min to hotel.
💡 Smart Tips
• Do NOT walk the entire street end-to-end — best food is in the first 50–60%
• If kids get tired: exit early → dessert at Kai Kai instead
• Bring: wet wipes · small cash bills · water
🇭🇰 Hong Kong Street Food Guide
Must-Try Famous HK Street Foods
🧇
Egg Waffles (Gai Daan Jai) 👍 Kid Favorite Crispy outside, fluffy inside waffle balls · Flavours: original, chocolate, matcha
🐟
Curry Fish Balls Bouncy fish balls in spicy curry sauce · Cheap, everywhere, very local · Mild spicy — ok for most kids
🍍
Pineapple Bun with Butter (Bo Lo Bao) Sweet bun, crunchy top — no pineapple inside! Best with butter 🧈 · Perfect breakfast or snack
🍢
Siu Mai (Street Version) Small dumplings — different from dim sum version · Served with soy sauce + chili
🧀
Stinky Tofu (For the Brave 😄) Fermented tofu, deep fried · Strong smell 😅 but surprisingly good!
🦑
Grilled Seafood & Skewers Squid · Scallops · Meat skewers · Very common in night markets 🔥
🍜
Cheung Fun (Rice Noodle Rolls) Soft rice rolls with sweet + peanut sauce · Super smooth texture
🍲
Claypot Rice ⭐ Must-Try at Night Markets Rice cooked in clay pot with toppings · Best part: crispy bottom!
🥭
Mango Sago Dessert Sweet mango + tapioca · So refreshing after salty food
📍 Where to Find These

🛍️ Mong Kok — best variety + street snacks (egg waffles, siu mai, cheung fun)

Quick Reference

Travel Essentials 📌

Getting Around
🚇
MTR (Subway)
Fastest & cheapest way around Hong Kong. Get an Octopus Card for easy tapping.
🚖
Taxis
Red taxis cover urban areas. Metered and generally affordable. Have hotel address in Chinese.
⛴️
Star Ferry
Scenic & cheap crossing between Kowloon and HK Island. One of the world's best ferry rides!
🚠
Peak Tram
Historic tram to Victoria Peak. Book tickets online to skip long queues.
Hotels
🏰 Disney Explorer's Lodge (Days 1–3)
📍 Lantau Island, near Disneyland
Great themed hotel with gardens, playground, pool. ~15–20 min taxi from airport.
🏙️ BP International (Days 3–7)
📍 Jordan, Kowloon
Central location for city exploration. Near Temple Street Night Market. ~25–30 min from Disneyland.

Money Tips 💰
💳
Currency: HKD (Hong Kong Dollar)
Get an Octopus Card for MTR, buses, ferries — and even 7-Eleven! Exchange cash at the airport or licensed money changers in the city (avoid hotel rates). Most malls accept cards.
Weather & Packing ☀️
👜
Bring comfortable walking shoes (lots of walking!), light layers for air-conditioned malls, sunscreen & hats for outdoor days, and a compact umbrella. Stay hydrated — especially on the Ngong Ping / Tai O day!
